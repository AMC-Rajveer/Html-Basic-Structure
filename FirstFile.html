Adamjee Cantonment Public School (ACPS) is one of the leading educational institutions in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Located within the Adamjee Cantonment area, the school operates under the management of the Bangladesh Army. It is renowned for its disciplined academic environment and high-quality education from primary to higher secondary levels.
